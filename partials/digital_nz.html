Search Digital New Zealand for a term or terms and view the metadata associated with your search. You can view the metadata in various ways, either as treemap or as force directed bubble chart. Click either chart type to zoom in further.

The available metadata fields are: category, creator, language and year. Metadata fields are based on which fields are facetable from the provider to reduce querying time.
