Search the DPLA for a term or terms and view the metadata associated with your search. The metadata is shown as force directed text cloud.

Double click either chart type to zoom in further. Use your scroll wheel to pan back out. Hover over a text bubble to view info about it. Click a text bubble to search the DPLA for your original term plus the clicked metadata field.

The available metadata fields are: country, format, language, provider, state and subject. Metadata fields are based on which fields are facetable from the provider to reduce querying time.
