Search Europeana for a term or terms and view the metadata associated with your search. The metadata is shown as force directed text cloud.

Double click either chart type to zoom in further. Use your scroll wheel to pan back out. Drag the visualization to move around in it. Hover over a text bubble to view info about it. Click a text bubble to search Europeana for your original term plus the clicked metadata field.

The available metadata fields are: country, provider, language, rights, type and year. Metadata fields are based on which fields are facetable from the provider to reduce querying time.
