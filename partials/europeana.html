You can sub select graph elements. In the smaller line graph click and drag the mouse to select a range. This will automatically scale the other graphs to just the highlighted elements. Clicking inside the graph, but outside the highlighted area will clear your selection.

In the bar charts hovering over a column will display the total for that column. Clicking a column will filter all graphs to show just information within that range. Clicking the column again will remove it from the selection.

The map works the same way. Click a column to filter all charts on uses just from that state.
